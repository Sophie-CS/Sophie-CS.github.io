Pier 17 Events

Tall Ship Danmark

During Climate Week in 2022, Danmark, a full-rigged large vessel with 3 masts and a steel hull, visits the U.S and docks at Pier 17. Danish cadets gain seafaring experience on this ship. This visit occured because Danmark had previously visited the U.S. during important periods, including World War II.

The Pumpkin Arch

A beautiful pumpkin arch is built every fall in variations at Pier 17, creating a fantastical appearance.

"MOMbies" Flash Mob: Halloween Dance

The MOMbies organization is made up of a large group of mothers and grandmothers that dance in efforts to fund research for metastatic breast cancer. In mid-October of 2024, they visited Pier 17 dressed as zombies and danced in formation to multiple pop songs in the sqaure.
